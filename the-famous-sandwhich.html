The Turkey and Cheese Sandwich

Description

This sandwich is a quick solution to anybody in between meetings or on the go. We have perfectly crafted a explosion in your mouth with sweet, tangy and overall flavor!

Ingredients

  • Killer Daves Bread
  • Mayo
  • Sweet and Spicy Mambo sauce
  • Bread and Butter pickles
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Turkey
  • Lettuce
  • Slice of Red Onion
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Toast two slices of bread
  2. Squeeze the condiments: Mayo and Mambo sauce on one side of each piece of bread
  3. Grab a knife and spread the condiments evenly on the bread
  4. Place four pickles on the one side of the bread
  5. Grab two slices of cheese and place on one side of each piece of bread
  6. Roll up two pieces of Turkey and place on top of the cheese
  7. Place all the remaining added Ingredients: Lettuce, Tomato, and Onions on one side of the bread
  8. Close the sandwich and ENJOY!
