The Spicy Avocado Toast

Description

This breakfast sandwich is a quick solution to anybody with limited time and that needs something yummy, packed with all the good stuff!

OR if you just finished a workout and need a snack to refuel your energy this is for you!

Ingredients

  • Killer Daves Bread
  • Mayo
  • Sweet and Spicy Mambo sauce
  • Two egs
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Avocado
  • Black Pepper
  • Red Pepper
  • Salt
  • Oregeno

Steps

  1. Toast two slices of bread
  2. Squeeze the condiments: Mayo and Mambo sauce on one side of each piece of bread
  3. Grab a knife and spread the condiments evenly on the bread
  4. Grab one slices of cheese and place on one side of each piece of bread
  5. Place a fried egg on the slice of bread
  6. Place all the remaining added Seasonings on top: black pepper, red pepper, salt and oregeno on one side of the bread with the fried egg
  7. Close the sandwich and ENJOY!
